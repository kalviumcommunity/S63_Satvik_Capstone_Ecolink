About Amur Leopards

The Amur Leopard is one of the world's most endangered big cats.

  • They are the northernmost subspecies of leopard
  • Their thick fur helps them survive in cold climates
  • They can run at speeds up to 37 miles per hour

Natural Habitat

Amur Leopards are found in:

  • Russian Far East
  • Northeast China
  • North Korea

Conservation Status

Only about 100 Amur Leopards remain in the wild.

  • Protected areas and wildlife corridors
  • Anti-poaching measures
  • Habitat restoration
